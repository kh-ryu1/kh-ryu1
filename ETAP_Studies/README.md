# ⚡ ETAP & Power System Analysis

Practical engineering training and technical capability development using **ETAP (Electrical Transient Analyzer Program)** for industrial electrical power system modeling, simulation, and analysis.

Studies performed based on international engineering standards for system design validation, protection coordination, equipment safety assessment, and electrical system performance evaluation.

---

## What I Can Do with ETAP

* Short Circuit Analysis *(IEC 60909, ANSI/IEEE C37)*
* Load Flow Analysis *(Newton-Raphson, Fast-Decoupled)*
* Protection Coordination Study *(Relay & Breaker Selectivity)*
* Arc Flash Analysis *(IEEE 1584, NFPA 70E)*
* Ground Grid System Study *(Step & Touch Voltage)*
* Motor Acceleration Analysis
* Transient Stability Analysis

---

## Engineering Applications

* Power system design review & validation
* Equipment rating and protective device coordination
* Electrical safety evaluation
* Grounding system performance verification
* Troubleshooting and system optimization
* Support for industrial, marine, offshore & LNG facilities
